FEATURES

Touch To Print, Just Like On A Tablet
Innovation and usability is built into the SMART MultiCpress K4300 Series.
The Samsung SMART UX Centre is the first smart Android printing UC that is intuitive, extendible and ensures compatibility with various Android-based devices.
Enjoy Preloaded Applications And Widgets
It’s all about convenience and flow. Your favourite applications such as Copy, Scan / Send, Box, Job Status, Address Book and Help come preloaded while custom widgets provide instant access to frequently used functions.
Faster Scanning And Fuss-free Printing
The SMART MultiXpress K4300 Series printer are equipped with Dual-Scan ADF technology that delivers faster scanning speeds of up to 80 single-sided image per minute(ipm) and up to 100 double side ipm for color and up to 120 double-side ipm for mono. And a 1 GHz dual-core CPU that powers printing at 30 pages per minute (ppm). With high-performance speed printing, businesses can work smoothly and operate more efficiently. (K4350LX, K4300LX Only)
Stand Out With Sharp, High-quality Print Output
Samsung’s unique Rendering Engine for Clean Page (ReCP) technology delivers sharper text and cleaner images. A superior resolution of 1,200 x 1,200 effective dpi ensures that everything you do will stand out.
Save resources with low TCO printing
Reduce labor costs for heavy printing with ultra-high-yield toner cartridges and drums. Toners that yield 20,000 standard pages for color to 23,000 for B&W pages and drums that yield 100,000 will lower your TCO and reduce waste.
Expand Use With Independent Functions And Extensibility
The interface also provides the Real Preview function from preloaded applications such as Copy, Send and Box. You can also get direct web access through the Android™ web browser to quickly print web content without a PC.
Personalise Your Experience Through My Page
Modify the My Page settings and Quick Menu for each user to keep track of an individual’s usage, control accessibility and more. Administrators can customise the UI by creating quick custom apps directly on the devices.
Create The Printing Environment That Suits Your Needs With Partner Solutions.
Numerous certified partner solutions are available through the Samsung XOA platform for further customisation.
Samsung’s Business Core™ Printing Solutions (BCPS)
Samsung’s Business Core™ Printing Solutions (BCPS) work as efficiently as you do with secure, cost-effective print operations. These solutions require no dedicated server or IT resources and reduces unnecessary printing and supply use.
Samsung Wireless & NFC Printing Option
With Samsung Wireless & NFC printing, you can simply tap your smart device to access various mobile printing solutions including user authentication, secure printing, easy wireless setup and device cloning.
Configurable Devices For Optimised Performance
The SMART MultiXpress K4300 Series printer are unique configuration can accommodate businesses of all sizes and industries.
Factory-provided modules serve as the foundation for adding various components and customising with optional components.
