FEATURES

Powerful quad-core CPU
With the industry’s first quad-core 1.5 GHz CPU with System-on-Chip technology delivering high-performance interpreting and rendering emulation, these powerful printers are twice as fast as conventional dual-core-based MFPs.
Two times faster dual scanning
The Dual-Scan Document Feeder scans both sides of a document simultaneously, capable of scanning up to 240 ipm, which is twice the scanning speed of the X4300 series. (GX Series Only)
Work SMART, just like on a tablet
The industry’s first Android™-based printing UX, the Samsung SMART UX Centre 2.0 functions like a tablet with an intuitive touch-to-print full HD 2560 x 1600 dpi resolution display with a 90-degree pivot.
Technology that ensures a crisper, clearer results
A Laser Beam Scanning Unit (LSU) produces a 1200 x 1200 dpi resolution and a next-generation polymerised toner ensures the best finishes while Rendering Engine for Clean Page (ReCP) technology sharpens graphics and text.
Reduce costs with high durability
Best-in-class maximum monthly duty means high durability, ensuring cost reduction and freedom from trivial work typically spent changing toner cartridges, drums and a developer.
Enhanced productivity with variable options
Boost productivity with a 2250-sheet booklet finisher and a 3250-sheet high-volume finisher that automatically staples up to 65 sheets. Print up to 6140 pages with a side and bottom High-Capacity Feeder HCF).
Do more with productivity boosting features
Easily set up printers by downloading essential apps from the Printing App Centre. Productivity tools include Workbook Composer, Hancom Office, Smart Colour Manager with more to be added. http://printingapps.samsung.com
Tap to print with NFC technology
Simply tap to print, scan or fax documents with advanced NFC technology from any supported mobile device. The wireless option with Active NFC provides user authentication through a mobile device.
Numerous options for the finishing touch
From a Booklet Finisher to Job Separator, a wide range of paper output options help boost productivity.
Input options to fit every need
Equip your office with various input cabinets that can accommodate print jobs of any size.
