USAGE TRACKER

Overview
Usage Tracker is a Samsung Printing innovation that controls printing expenses by easily tracking device functions. ※ HDD installation is recommend for printers.
Printing Cost Tracking
Keep your printing usage and costs in check by tracking all devices and users within your network with ease. Monitor a specific function of one printer, or track various outputs from multiple devices within your network. With the Usage Tracker tool, you can track printing, copying, scanning and faxing counts-all without a server.
Proactive Monitoring
Keep operating costs at a minimum by reviewing printer usage and forecasting expenses. Email notifications help you monitor device usage remotely, while IT managers can aggregate reports on up to 10 devices(For XOA-E), 12 devices (For XOA-E&XOA-Web).
Wide Range Offering
Even without a separate server, A4 mid-high MFPs and printers can utilize a variety of solutions through the XOA-Web platform, in which the XOA-E model acts as a server. Enjoy the efficiency of one XOA-E model that can connect from up to five XOA-Web models. (XOA-E and XOA-Web models can accommodate up to 12 devices.)

SECUTHRU™ LITE2

Overview
Be smart and secured when it comes to managing workflow with Samsung SecuThru™ Lite 2. Safeguard confidential documents and company data with secure authentication and release functions. Plus, you can reduce costs through pull printing and eliminating unclaimed print jobs.
Secure Authentication and Release
Bring enterprise-level security to your business with Samsung SecuThru™ Lite2 which provides secure authentication and release of documents. When a user sends a document to a printer, all jobs are temporarily and securely stored in the device. The document is released only when the authorized user provides authentication.
Secure Pull Printing
Share print jobs knowing confidential documents are delivered securely and exclusively to authorized users. SecuThru™ Lite 2 ensures only authorized users can pull documents from up to 10 devices(For XOA-E), 12 devices(For XOA-E&XOA-WEB) connected to the network. Flexible authentication accommodates up to 500 users.
Reduced Printing Costs
Reduce printing costs in more ways than one. Give printer access to authorized users and eliminate unclaimed printouts through pull printing capabilities. Best of all, there’s no need for an additional server.
Wide Range Offering
Even without a separate server, A4 mid-high MFPs and printers are now capable of secure pull printing through the XOA-Web platform, in which the XOA-E model acts as its own server. It makes your job more convenient and your business more economical.

SECURE LOGIN MANAGER

Overview
Secure Login Manager is a Samsung Printing Innovation that protects valuable business from unauthorized use with several different methods of secure authentication. ※ Secure Login Manager supports MFP only
Flexible User Access
Granting printer access to a number of different users has never been more secure or flexible. Secure Login Manager supports a variety of authentication processes for user access, including unique ID and passwords, proximity cards and a Personal Identification Number (PIN).
Secure Authentication
Protect valuable devices from unauthorized use with several different methods of secure authentication
Wide Range Offering

Enjoy the efficiency of one XOA-E model that can connect from up to five XOA-Web models. (XOA-E and XOA-Web models can accommodate up to 12 devices.)

SMARTHRU™ WORKFLOW LITE

Overview
SmarThru™ Workflow Lite is a Samsung Printing Innovation that creates a seamless and intuitive infrastructure for digital document handling.
One-touch Scanning to Distribution
SmarThru™ Workflow Lite streamlines manual and repetitive work by distributing documents to multiple predefined destinations with a one-touch scan. Scanned documents are automatically sent to destinations such as email, shared folders and MS Sharepoint. It’s all done without a server so you’ll always have easy access to the files you need.
Zone-OCR and Varcode Recognition
In addition to Barcode recognition, SmarThru™ Workflow Lite has Zone OCR recognition, an optical character recognition feature that reads and translates scanned character images into character codes. Once captured, documents can be routed to predetermined destinations based on barcode scans or text content. Save resources and increase productivity with additional built-in automation such as file naming and folder creation.
Fax Routing
Enjoy the convenience of having an incoming fax routed to a specific individual or a group. Enhance your workflow with automated Fax Routing which distributes incoming faxes to predetermined locations by manually categorizing locations using metadata or caller ID.
Wide Range Offering
Even without a separate server, A4 mid-high MFPs and printers are now capable of secure pull printing through the XOA-Web platform, in which the XOA-E model acts as its own server. It makes your job more convenient and your business more economical.

CLOUD CONNECTOR

Overview
Cloud Connector is a Samsung Printing innovation which allows users to scan to and print from the cloud with easy, secure access without an additional server.
Easy, Secure Access to the Cloud
Users can directly scan and print from the cloud with Samsung Cloud Connector. The Cloud Connector supports a variety of Cloud services and provides an easy and secure way to upload, scan and access content for printing directly without an additional server.
Support for Various Formats & Services
More supported files and services mean less work for you. Cloud Connector accepts PDF, TIFF and XPS scans, and is able to print from PDF and Google™ Drive® and Hightail. With the ability to support more services including Microsoft™ SharePoint® Online and Google™ Drive®, Cloud Connector allows you to focus on what matters the most.
