FIDELIA, Huge response at European market exhibiting ‘Mode City PARIS 2012’2012.07.13



- CEO of EUROBET for event host wins high praise for “Surprised for FIEDLIA’s creative design and passion”
- Rushing a love call of European market on the first exhibition for collection line of creative director ‘Cilia Boes’



 FIDELIA wins high praise at ‘Mode City PARIS 2012’, the international lingerie and swimsuit expo opened in Paris, France from 7th to 9th. In ‘Mode City PARIS 2012’, 770 companies in total including the material company attended with 550 companies of swimsuit brand and among them, FIDELIA is the only Korean brand.

FIDELIA exclusively prepares a booth for public relations in this event and exhibits 10 set works in total at main fashion show opening for various themes everyday for 3days. Especially, since for this entry, FIDELIA scouts Cilia Boes, a former top designer of CHRISTIAN DIOR, as a creative director and gains a public favor showing collection works on transcendentalizing FIDELIA’s women’s beauty based on handicraft technique learned by the sense of haute couture. After the main lingerie show, buyer and press who has interested in FIDELIA’s lingerie, gives a huge prosperity on booth for public relations.

Cecile Vivier Guerin, CEO of EUROBET for event host, said “As watching FIDELIA’s collection, I was surprised on creative ideas and passion which has a Korean brand” and said “I could feel an Asian new energy”. Jennifer Kell, a designer director of ‘concept Paris’ who leads the European lingerie market’s trend, extols “I could feel the French detail and the power of global brand who has a FIDELIA’s certain color in total areas including material and quality”.

FIDELIA exhibited 60 works in total including premium line ‘Vera Wang for FIDELIA’ as well as 30 works in total for first exhibited collection line under directing by Cilia Boes. In this collection works for Cilia Boes, we could meet at CJ O shopping since this FW season.
