Luxury of Dress, Vera Wang - Meeting “FIDELIA” Luxury of lingerie2012.03.20



- World famous designer Vera Wang cooperates with FIDELIA and shows off ‘Vera Wang for FIDELIA’
- Luxury of lingerie for women, Born for global collaboration of ‘FIDELIA’ in 2012



 Lingerie ‘FIDELIA’ of Designer collaboration concept collaborates with VERA WANG, luxury of dress industry and opens the showcase celebrating the launching at Cheongdam Beyond Musuem on 27th.

FIDELIA shows the new line with the new name ‘Vera Wang for FIDELIA through the first global collaboration with Vera Wang, the world famous designer. ‘Vera Wang for FIDELIA’ is the premium lingerie which maximizes the beauty through the complete understanding for women’s body.

This collaboration shows the complete lingerie compressing the women’s beauty and stabilized function under the concept of ‘Beautiful Body solution’. Especially in between the celebrities at home and abroad, it catches one’s eye for cooperating with Vera Wang, a higher preference global top designer. It shows the luxurious items through common denominator for elegant feminine which could be found in lingerie and dress.

FIDELIA’s authority mentions that ‘Above all things, lots of people’s attention would be expected for first collaboration with Vera Wang, luxury brand in dress industry’ and tells that FIEDELIA expects to open a new prospect for lingerie adding with the know-how of premium lingerie brand and the elegant from Vera Wang.

FIDELIA is the first domestic designer lingerie brand and has the characteristics on departmentalized product line setting with concept and function. After the first launching of ‘Lee Shin-woo’ line in 2001, FIDELIA establish a solid foothold for brand collaborating with ‘Michel Sharrier’ and “Park Yoon-jung”.
