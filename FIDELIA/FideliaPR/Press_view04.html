Successful Venture by Korean Lingerie Brand, ‘Fidelia’, Seducing Paris2012.01.21



- The success of an unrivaled lingerie brand with new sales channels, online and mobile, has caught the attention of Europe.
- Following collaborations with Michel Charrier, Gisele Bundchen, and Designer Vera Wang, Fidelia has recruited designer Cilia Boёs, the former lingerie designer of Christian Dior, to head its creative design team.



 Fidelia, celebrating its 12th anniversary, is introducing its elegant lingerie lines at the international lingerie show for the fourth time since 2002. Through this trade show, Fidelia is once again impressing the European market with its abundant global experience and know-how.

In Mode City Paris 2013, Fidelia will showcase a total of 12 styles. In pursuit of European sensibility, the brand has reinterpreted Haute Couture, showcasing sophisticated and sensual designs.

Following the collaboration with designer Vera Wang in 2012, the brand has nominated Cilia Boes who is the former Christian Dior lingerie designer, as the creative director, spurring the growth of the brand as it makes the leap to a global brand.

Fidelia’s products showcased in the Mode City Lingerie Show last year were well received and resulted in high sales in Korea. Korean consumers are fascinated by Cilia Boes’ unique and gorgeous embroidery and patterns inspired from Parisian architecture.

The 2013 release of Fidelia’s new lingerie lines is expected to be another great success.
